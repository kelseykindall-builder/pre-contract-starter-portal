# Starter Portal: Sprint Plan v4 — Three Landing Pages

**Sprint Focus:** Split the v3 portal into three audience-specific landing pages, each tuned to where the starter is in the funnel
**Date:** May 13, 2026
**Owner:** Kelsey Kindall
**Status:** Planning · Page 1 in active build

---

## The Scope Shift From v3

v3 was a two-page portal for starters who'd already signed a contract: a personalized welcome screen + a petition topic picker + share unit submission. It assumed the starter was in.

v4 zooms out and recognizes there are **three distinct moments** in the starter's journey, each with different motivations, different unknowns, and different decision points:

1. **Pre-contract** — they've heard about the program, they're interested, they haven't committed. The page's job is to motivate them to sign.
2. **Petition creation** — they've signed, they need to launch their petition.
3. **Share unit** — petition is live, time to post the share units and submit URLs.

v3 collapsed all three moments into two pages assuming the starter was already past pre-contract. v4 separates them so each page can do its specific job well.

---

## The Three Pages

### Page 1: Pre-Contract Landing — IN ACTIVE BUILD
**Audience:** A starter who has received the outreach email and clicked through to learn more. They have not signed a contract. They don't know their advance amount yet.

**CTA:** "Sign your contract"

**Job:** Make them feel that this is a legitimate, well-paid, low-friction opportunity that's *different* from a typical brand deal — and that they can keep their voice while getting paid.

**Constraint:** Must be advance-amount agnostic. The starter's tier (and therefore advance) is confirmed during contract negotiation, not displayed up front. The page can show the *range* of advances across tiers, but never names a specific dollar amount as "yours."

**Content:**
- Welcome hero ("Get paid to spotlight what you care about")
- 3-min Kelsey overview video with Overview block + four-question grid
- Deliverables stat grid (advance shown as range/tier-based, not specific)
- "Why this isn't a brand deal" differentiators
- Earnings calculator showing only promotions revenue (no advance addition)
- Tier preview chart
- Proof points / recent wins
- 3-step "how it works"
- Footer CTA — Sign your contract + Book a call with Kelsey

## What Carries Over From v3

- Design system (Sunrise tokens, Commissioner font, red-as-punctuation, yellow CTA)
- Tokenized URL pattern (`?starter=<token>`) for personalization
- Admin dashboard + share unit submission backend
- Reward-framed urgency (not deadline-pressure)

## What's New in v4

- Three pages instead of two, addressing three distinct funnel moments
- Pre-contract page advance-agnostic, focuses on motivation + differentiation rather than personalized deal
- Petition creation page (separate from welcome) gives the topic picker its own dedicated screen
- Share unit page becomes its own destination (was bundled with topic picker in v3)

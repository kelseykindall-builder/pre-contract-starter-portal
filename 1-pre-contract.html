3 is the floor — not the ceiling. You need 3 to keep your advance, but the program has no cap on posts. The more you share, the more signatures you drive. The more signatures, the more promotions your petition will receive — and the more you earn each month.

Each share must be unique creative — original to that post, not a repost of another share unit. Reposting the same clip across other platforms is encouraged for visibility, but it doesn't count as a new share unit. Every post must include #ad or #sponsored somewhere in the caption.

Why this isn't a typical brand deal

Built for creators, not commodified by them

Your voice, all the way through

You pick the topic. You write the petition. You post in your style. Our team helps shape it for maximum impact and reach — but the message is ultimately yours. The only constraint: your petition must comply with our community guidelines.

Guaranteed payment, no signature target

We activate your advance the moment you share your first post — paid out within 30 days. You keep it whether your petition gets 1,000 signatures or 100,000. Performance isn't a gate — it's an opportunity to earn more.

No earnings ceiling

Once your petition recoups your advance, you start earning 95% of promotions revenue — every month, as long as it keeps gaining signatures. Most brand deals are one-and-done. This one compounds.

The chance to keep going

Once your petition's promotions revenue surpasses your advance, you unlock the option to start another one — fresh earnings stacking on top of the petition you already have running. Top creators have turned this into a steady second income stream. (Not guaranteed — we offer it to ambassadors who've shown they can mobilize their audience.)
